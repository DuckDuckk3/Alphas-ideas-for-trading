# VI. Regime and State
## 1. Volatility Regime “market state”
### Idea: market behavior depends on volatility
- High vol → chaos
- Low vol → stability
### Typical form:
`ts_std_dev(returns,n)`
### Insight:
- Used to adjust signals
- Not standalone alpha
## 2. Trend Regime “context matters”
### Idea: different strategies for different markets
- Trending → momentum
- Sideways → mean reversion
### Typical form:
`ts_sum(returns, long_horizon)`
### Insight:
- Determines which signal to trust
## 3. Conditional Activation “only trade when good”
### Idea: act only when signal quality is high
### Typical form:
`signal * condition`
### Insight:
- Reduces noise
- Improves robustness
