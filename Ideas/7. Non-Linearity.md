# VII. Non-linearity (very strong to reduce self-correlation)
## 1. Interaction Effects “signals reinforce”
### Idea: combining signals non-linearly
### Typical form:
`A * B`
### Insight:
- Creates stronger edge
- Avoid redundant signals
## 2. Asymmetry -> “up ≠ down”
### Idea: positive and negative moves behave differently
### Typical form:
`ts_sum(max(returns,0),n)` or 
`ts_sum(min(returns,0),n)`
### Insight:
- Captures market psychology
- Important in crashes
