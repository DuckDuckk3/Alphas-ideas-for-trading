# V. Time and Decay
## 1. Time Horizon “scale matters”
### Idea: same signal behaves differently at different horizons
### Typical form:
`ts_sum(returns, short / mid / long)`
### Insight:
- Short = noisy
- Medium = optimal
- Long = stable but lagging
## 2. Decay “recent info matters more”
### Idea: newer data is more relevant
### Typical form:
`ts_decay_linear(signal,n)`
### Insight:
- Reduces noise
- Controls turnover
