# GENERAL IDEAS THAT SHAPES EVERY ALPHA

## Some ideas that many people follow: 
- Momentum: Assume that stocks that have outperformed in the pass with continue to outperform in the future, while stocks that have performed poorly will continue to do so (This effect may appear over longer periods).
- Reversion: If something increases today, it will fall tomorrow. And if something decreases today, it will increase tomorrow (this effect may appear over shorter periods)
