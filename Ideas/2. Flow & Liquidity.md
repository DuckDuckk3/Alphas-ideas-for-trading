# II. Flow & Liquidity 
## 1. Volume Level - “attention signal”
### Idea: unusual volume = new information
High volume → strong participation
### Typical form:
`volume / ts_mean(volume,n)`
### Insight:
- Used as confirmation
- Not strong alone
- Helps pass liquidity constraints
## 2. Price–Volume Interaction - “conviction”
### Idea: price move + volume = strength
- Up + high volume → real buying
- Down + high volume → real selling
### Typical form:
`volume * returns`
`(volume / ts_mean(volume,n)) * returns`
### Insight:
- Filters fake moves
- Adds conviction to signals
## 3. Liquidity Imbalance — “pressure”
### Idea: imbalance between buyers and sellers
- High volume + up → demand
- High volume + down → supply
### Typical form:
`rank(volume / ts_mean(volume,n)) * rank(returns)`
### Insight:
- Captures flow direction
- Strong in trend formation
