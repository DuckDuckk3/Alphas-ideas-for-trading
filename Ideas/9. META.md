# IX. Meta
## 1. Multi-Horizon “layer information”
### Idea: combine different time scales
### Typical structure:
- short → entry
- mid → signal
- long → context
### Insight:
- Core of strong alphas
## 2. Orthogonality “independent signals”
### Idea: different signals = different edges
### Insight:
- Avoid stacking similar signals
- Diversity > quantity
## 3. Simplicity “less is more”
### Idea: simple models generalize better
### Insight:
- Fewer operators → more robust
- Overfitting kills performance
## 4. Turnover Control “don’t overtrade”
### Idea: too much trading = instability
### Typical tools:
- longer horizon
- smoothing
### Insight:
- Critical for passing constraints
- Stability > reactivity
